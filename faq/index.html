Frequently Asked Questions

Updated on June 22, 2024

What is “Woke”?

The woke movement is complex, but it can be summarized by one idea: everything is political. Woke ideas revolve around a multitude of topics such as cultural relativism, post-structuralism, postcolonialism, and postmodernism.

Why is it bad?

When political topics and agendas are heavily integrated into games, it can disrupt the escapism and enjoyment that many players seek.

What makes a game woke?

We’ve gathered some elements that are often present when it is applied in games.

  • “Mary Sue” Characters: Perfect, flawless characters, often female
  • Anti-White and Anti-Male: White men always portrayed as dumb and/or evil
  • Forced diversity and overrepresentation of sexual minorities
  • Pronoun Protocol
  • Character Uglification: Especially women characters
  • Censorship: Modifying or removing content deemed offensive or inappropriate
  • Historical Revisionism: Altering historical facts to fit modern perspectives
  • Oppressed and Oppressor: Framing characters in terms of oppression dynamics
  • Anti-Capitalism: Applying the marxists idea of capitalism
  • Anti-Religious: Especially a negative portrayal of Christianity
  • Moral Relativism: Portraying morality as subjective and culturally dependent
  • Anti-Traditional Family and Anti-Natalism
